WHO <AM> I?

Frontend development wasn’t love at first sight — more like frustration at first bug. But there was something satisfying about breaking things and then figuring out why they broke.

Now, it’s all about crafting smooth interfaces, arguing with CSS, negotiating with JavaScript. Code gets messy, the browser throws a fit, and somehow it all comes together.

There’s no long résumé or grand origin story here — just a lot of late nights, lots of tabs open, and a weird enjoyment of figuring things out the hard way. And yes, the struggle is part of the fun.
